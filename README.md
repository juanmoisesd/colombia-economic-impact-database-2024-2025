## Description

This database compiles key economic indicators and principal influencing factors for Colombia's economy during 2024-2025. Variables include: GDP growth rate, inflation rate, unemployment rate, oil production (bpd), official exchange rate (COP/USD), household poverty rate, foreign exchange reserves, public debt (% of GDP), trade balance, remittances, FDI inflows, and fiscal deficit.

Key influencing factors: Petro government fiscal and social policies, oil sector production decline, armed conflict and security, drug trafficking parallel economy, US-Colombia trade relations, dollarization debate, and agricultural export performance.

Data source: Perplexity AI aggregating from BBVA Research, Trading Economics, CEIC Data, Deloitte, Itau, Rystad Energy.
